Caves are graded (0-5) with respect to rope access difficulty, water related obstacles, climbing and tightness.

Rope (R):
  • 0: no rope needed
  • 1,2: simple rigging
  • 3,4: pullthroughs, traverses etc.
  • 5: tight pitchheads etc.
Water (W):
  • 0: dry cave
  • 1,2: dry bum
  • 3,4: waist deep water or lying in puddles
  • 5: requires diving
Climbing (C):
  • 0: no climbs
  • 1,2: some climbs, belay not needed
  • 3,4: some lead climbing
  • 5: lead climibing with grades 5a or above
Tightness (T):
  • 0: mostly walking
  • 1,2: occasional crawl
  • 3,4: large people might have difficulty
  • 5: black book trips
